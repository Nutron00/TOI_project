Insights:

  • Murder cases constitute 14% of all crimes, and an increase was observed in both 2022 and 2023.
  • This has been recorded that most of the crimes have happened in January and December.
  • Delhi and Mumbai have the highest crime rates and most of the cases are from these places.
  • Crime trended up, resulting in a 168.32% increase between 2001 and 2024.
  • Crime jumped from 1,382 to 3,251 during its steepest incline between 2010 and 2013.
  • Crime started trending up in 2014, rising by 108.38% (2,884) in 9 years.
